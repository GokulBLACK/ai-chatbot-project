# LexExplain — Bail Decision Justification & Legal Boundary Compliance Bot

***

## Overview

LexExplain is a full‑stack AI chatbot that translates judicial bail orders into neutral, structured legal logic. It explains bail as a risk‑management process, neutralizes emotional narratives, and strictly enforces legal boundaries — meaning it never gives legal advice, predicts outcomes, or determines guilt or innocence.

The system operates in two modes:
- **Legal Literacy Mode (Lex):** Rule‑based structured analysis of uploaded bail‑order JSON datasets.
- **Normal Chat Mode:** Conversational AI powered by Groq's free‑tier LLM (no credit card required).
